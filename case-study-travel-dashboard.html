Planning & Organization

Travel Planning Dashboard

A centralized Google Sheets dashboard built to organize international travel itineraries, accommodations, destinations, and schedules in one accessible place.

Scenario

Created a personal travel planning dashboard for my 2025 international trip to organize itineraries, accommodations, destinations, and schedules in one place.

Challenge

Build a centralized system that keeps all travel information organized, easy to access, and simple to update throughout the trip.

Tasks Completed

  • Designed a Google Sheets travel planning dashboard.
  • Created a day-by-day travel itinerary.
  • Organized accommodation details and hotel addresses.
  • Added tourist destinations and daily activities.
  • Integrated Google Maps links for easy navigation.
  • Structured travel information into a clean, easy-to-use dashboard.

Tools Used

Google Sheets Google Maps

Skills Demonstrated

Travel Planning Administrative Support Internet Research Data Organization Schedule Management Attention to Detail Documentation

Outcome

Developed a well-organized travel dashboard that demonstrates my ability to plan trips, manage information efficiently, and create clear, easy-to-follow travel itineraries that can be adapted for future clients.

How I Can Help Clients

I help clients plan and organize business or personal travel by creating detailed itineraries, researching accommodations, and keeping travel information organized in one place.
